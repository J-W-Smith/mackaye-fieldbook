# Privacy Model

Privacy levels for future sharing are Private, Approved Friends, Shared by Link, and Public Summary.

Potential comparisons may expose completion percentage, common sections, direction, season, total recorded mileage, and user-selected highlights/photos. Exact overnight coordinates, precise timestamps, private notes, and raw GPS tracks remain private by default.

Public community features require blocking, reporting, moderation, appeals, content controls, abuse response, and granular revocation before implementation.

Retention is local until the user edits, deletes, or exports it. “Delete all local data” removes user tables/settings/outbox but not bundled guide content.
